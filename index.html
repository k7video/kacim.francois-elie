Webpage of K. François-Elie

My name is Kacim François-Elie, and I am a PhD Student at CEA/Université Paris-Saclay, under the supervision of Alain Mazzolo and Simone Santandrea, working at SERMA (Department of Reactor Studies and Applied Mathematics) since November 2022.
I am a former student of the Ecole des Mines de Nancy where I specialized in Mathematical Engineering.
You can contact me at: firstname.name_initials@gmail.com.

Research interests

  • Theory and numerics of Boltzmann Transport Equation (BTE).
  • Convergence analysis and estimators.
  • Numerical methods for Partial Differential Equations (PDEs) and Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs).
  • Acceleration methods of numerical schemes.

    About my research

    I am currently studying the DPN acceleration method applied to the method of the characteristics, to solve the BTE for neutrons. This research topic is related to nuclear reactor physics, more specifically to lattice calculations.
